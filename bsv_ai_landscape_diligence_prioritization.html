Limitations

  1. The framework is useful for public-source triage, but it still relies on inferred signals rather than private traction, customer references, revenue quality, valuation, or founder diligence.
  2. The systems map shows plausible interdependence, but it does not quantify dependency strength, usage overlap, or actual portfolio synergies.
  3. The ranking is a diligence-priority view, not a final investment view, until tested against hands-on product work, buyer interviews, competitive depth, and funding context.
Source posture: This artifact uses public information from BSV portfolio/research pages and company-owned docs/sites. Company scores are prioritization judgments, not investment recommendations.
